About Me

Hello, my name is Christine. I have always been a student of life. But my deep dive into my life's purpose began in my 50’s.

I have experienced my fair share of adversity. Death, divorce, relocation *and, so much more.

The story that I want to share began in my 40’s. That is when I met the man I thought I would spend the rest of my life with. I was happy and deeply in love.

My clientele began to grow and soon after the opportunity to train other estheticians presented itself. I had finally found my calling for teaching and mentoring other women. But there was still something missing. As I delved deeper into myself, I realized I still wasn’t living life to my fullest potential. I wanted more. My focus suddenly shifted toward healing a healing that would be the biggest I had ever experienced. Although this was a tumultuous time for me, the split propelled me into an even deeper path of self-discovery. Therapy, journaling, friends, and a multitude of spiritual practices carried me through this uncertain time.

Miraculously, my life's path came to light after a trip to Hawaii for a HUNA Energy Training. It was there that the former Christine “died" figuratively speaking. I realized the former me, the person that I was, and the life behind me no longer served my soul. I was gifted a clean slate; a breath of a new life blew inside of me. I was reborn and began the process of creating a new life.

Astrology became yet another passion. As I started to read birth charts for my clients, I discovered yet another layer of gifts that had been lying dormant. Astrology allowed me the opportunity to dabble with coaching, which propelled me to pursue my passion & purpose of helping people find out “Who they authentically are” and what they want out of life ~ at any age. As a student of life I continued pursuing an education in personal growth and development, so I am able to help YOU grow and live your best life. I am here as a testament and invested partner who wants to guide you through what is calling you, while reminding you that it is NEVER too late to reclaim who you truly are and wish to be.

As a person of integrity, my studies and certifications were carefully selected to give you the utmost of care. I look forward to being a part of your journey.

What is NLP?

NLP or Neuro Linguistic Programming is a collection of tools and techniques that are used to create significant and lasting changes in people in a short period of time. NLP is typically used within coaching sessions as questions, exercises, and visualizations that can completely change the way someone thinks and behaves.

NLP is essentially a study of the connection between the neurological processes ("neuro"), language ("linguistic") and behavioral patterns learned through experience ("programming") and as such, these can be changed to achieve specific goals in life. All sessions are designed to create rapport and trust, so that you feel comfortable to open up and express what is really going on. NLP clients gain clarity for what they truly want and develop clear and concise goals that support their outcomes.

NLP can be used to establish empowering states of mind and to overcome any limiting beliefs, conflicts, and bad habits. It also can be used to break free of cravings or dislike your favorite junk food!

NLP will help to:

  • Reduce limiting beliefs and habits, replacing them with empowering ones
  • Be freed from the stress and worry of past negative experiences
  • Be more understanding of differences and enrich relationships with others
  • Feel more resourceful even in challenging situations
  • Enhance self-appreciation and self- esteem
  • Set appropriate, achievable goals to motivate you to know the path you wish to take in life and to plan your journey effectively
